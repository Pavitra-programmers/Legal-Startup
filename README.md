# NyayJustis

**Tagline** — *Because every woman deserves a witness.*

**Motto** — *No woman should face the law alone.*

---

## What We're Building

NyayJustis is India's first real-time legal protection platform for women. One tap connects a citizen to a verified advocate via live video during police stops, traffic encounters, and Motor Vehicles Act disputes — in under 90 seconds.

The moment she presses SOS:
- Recording starts immediately (audio, video, GPS timestamp)
- A verified advocate joins the live video call within 90 seconds
- The advocate advises her in real time — what to say, what not to sign, what is lawful
- Everything is timestamped and witnessed
- She receives a court-admissible evidence package certified under Section 65B of the Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam 2023

Payment uses **NyayPoints** — a pre-loaded wallet (bought before any emergency) so there is zero payment friction at the moment of stress.

## Goals

**Phase 1 — Shield**
Launch in 5 metro cities with 1,000 verified advocates. Prove the SOS model for non-cognizable offences and Motor Vehicles Act disputes. Android-first. Hindi, Hindustani, Urdu, and English.

**Phase 2 — Intelligence**
Expand to 20 cities. Launch Nyaya LLM — an AI assistant trained on Indian MVA, BNSS, and landmark Supreme Court judgments. 12 vernacular languages. Advocate Pro SaaS and NyayJustis Premium subscription.

**Phase 3 — Infrastructure**
Partner with the National Human Rights Commission and Bar Council of India. Launch Nyaya Mirror — an anonymised public dashboard of encounter patterns and rights-violation hotspots. Become the foundational infrastructure of India's access-to-justice movement.
